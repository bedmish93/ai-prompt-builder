Prompt secrets that actually work

Specificity wins: Vague requests = garbage output. Be ruthlessly specific about what you want.
Context is king: Feed the AI the backstory. It can't read your mind (yet).
Format rules everything: Want a table? Say table. Want bullets? Say bullets. Don't leave it to chance.
Show, don't tell: One good example beats ten vague descriptions. Show what awesome looks like.
Set boundaries: No limits = chaos. Define length, style, what to avoid. Be the boss.
Iterate ruthlessly: First prompt sucks? Good. Refine it until it's perfect. Winners iterate.
